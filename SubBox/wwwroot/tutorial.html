SubBox - Profile

In the profile tab, you can choose your username and make notes about whatever you need. See when you first started using SubBox. Every day a new background image appears.

SubBox - Manage Your Channels

Add your favourite channels through entering their channel title. Through the channels button you can get a list of all your currently added channels. Here you can decide to show only videos of one particular channel by clicking on the star icon. Remove a channel with the delete button.

Don't know what a button does? Hover over it with your Mouse to display a tooltip.

SubBox - Add Playlists

Add playlists you want to watch by simply entering the respective playlist id. To get the playlist id, open the playlist on Youtube and copy the id from the link. You will always see each current video of every playlist in your list marked with the PLAYLIST box. Use the arrow buttons on the playlist video to go to the next/previous video or use the list button to get an overview over the whole playlist. You want to combine playlists? Just click the list button on an already created playlist and use the add button.

SubBox - Using Playlists

If you don't want you playlists to clutter your front page, you can hide them in the playlist tab. This way you can add playlists that you want to watch in a few months time.

You can search for videos in your SubBox by simply typing anywhere.

SubBox - The Trashbin

If you removed videos from your list on accident or you changed your mind, you can just click on the trashbin icon to get a list of all removed videos. Be aware these videos will be permanently deleted after they have reached a certain age. You can set this age yourself, more on that later.

SubBox - Search and Tags

Hover over the "SubBox" to use the search bar. If one search keyword isn't enough, you can make use of tags. Just create a tag and add all keywords you want to combine. When selecting a tag, all videos, containing at least one of the keywords, will be shown.

SubBox - Customize To Your Needs

Bright themes hurt your eyes? You can switch to a more comfortable dark theme with just one click. Red is nice and all but you prefer different colours? Switch to any accent colour of your choice in the settings tab. Set the time spans in which videos are retrieved and deleted. Smart List Loading is a feature that helps you improve performance when you have a lot of videos in your SubBox at the cost of small stuttering on scrolling.

SubBox - Nigthly

Download Videos in your SubBox and watch them later from your Harddrive without the need for Internet. The desired Video Quality for your downloads can be set in the Settings Menu. Individual Videos outside of your SubBox can be downloaded with the console command "-dlsv".

Check out SubBox on Github to not miss future updates!
